What is FHE?

Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) is a way of encrypting data in such a way that it can be processed (e.g., added, multiplied) in encrypted form without having to decrypt it. This allows you to perform calculations on encrypted data without revealing its contents, which ensures a high level of confidentiality when processing information.
